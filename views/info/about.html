About Calagora

Calagora is a silent-auction website, inspired in part by Tinder (we're not kidding!), dedicated to helping you buy and sell used college supplies to other students on your campus, helping you save money.

Calagora provides you with the ability to post listings that you would like to sell to other users on your campus. It also lets you explore listings that have been posted by other users on your campus, and make offers to buy those listings.

As a seller, Calagora allows you to decide which offers you would like to reply to, and which offers you'd rather not. If you decide to accept an offer, Calagora opens up a chat client between you and your buyer so that you can discuss meeting up and facilitating an in-person transaction.
